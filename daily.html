Locked Eyes and Likability

Senior Capstone Research Project, Tufts University - 2019

Skills Used

  • Data Analysis

Tools Used

  • Tableau
  • Voyant
  • Word trees

This was a project for Enigma, an independent, student-run data journal at Tufts University. As inclusivity and the idea of gender as a non-binary concept have become more visible in recent years, especially on college campuses, I wanted to examine whether or not these cultural shifts were represented in language. Media is both a representation of the current state of language and a tool for changing language. Using the archives of the Tufts Daily independent newspaper, I examined the use of gender pronouns over multiple decades.
