About Me


I'm fascinated by emerging technologies and their impact on individual users as well as society at large. I graduated from Tufts University with a B.S. in Cognitive and Brain Sciences as well as minors in Computer Science and English.


My interdisciplinary background as well as my previous work in both academia and startup spaces inspired my interest in human-technology interaction. I am fascinated by the way that technology shapes human behavior and changes the way people interact with one another. I hope to continue my education and learn how to create technology that is not only pleasant to interact with, but can spark lasting positive change – and do so with ethics and wellbeing in mind. I am hopeful for a future in which the "Screen Time" on our phones is a small number compared to something like "Outdoor Time" or "In Person Conversation Time."
